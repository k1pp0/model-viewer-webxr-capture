@k1pp0/model-viewer-webxr-capture — Example

Press the AR button, place the model on a flat surface, then tap the shutter button to capture a composited image. The ar-screenshot event fires with a Blob that can be shared via the Web Share API or downloaded directly.

Requires a WebXR-capable device (Android + Chrome). On iOS or unsupported devices the experience gracefully falls back to Quick Look or Scene Viewer.
